REPRESENTATION
BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Our Board brings a variety of skills and experiences to their roles and represent diversity in the broadest sense of the word, including age, gender, race/ethnicity, industry, discipline, and specialized experience – all to help us win in the marketplace. This balanced and representative leadership helps us navigate a dynamic, complex, and competitive environment in markets around the globe.

Jon Moeller

Chairman of the Board
President and Chief Executive Office

For many years, P&G has taken pride in leveraging the diversity of our Board, our management team, and our employees to help us win in the marketplace.  We are proud of our progress and recognize there is still more we can do.


P&G is a global consumer products company, operating in a complex, competitive global environment. Our Directors possess seasoned judgment and agility to oversee emerging risks and challenges. We seek leaders with strong governance acumen and diverse expertise in global business, aligned with our Company’s Purpose, Values, and Principles.
