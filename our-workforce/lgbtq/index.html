GLOBAL WORKFORCE

LGBTQ+

For more than 30 years, P&G has worked to create a welcome and supportive workplace for LGBTQ+ employees. We value unique perspectives and experiences which not only make us better colleagues, but also a better company. Our LGBTQ+ employees enable us to unlock insights about the range of consumers our brands serve — helping us to be more inclusive and discover more about the world around us.

Alex Keith

Chief Executive Officer - P&G Beauty
Executive Sponsor - LGBTQ+ Equality
Executive Sponsor - Corporate Sustainability

We're dedicated to fostering an environment where 100% of our employees can be their authentic selves 100% of the time. Through self-identification, we enable holistic representation. Our active LGBTQ+ community, along with allies, creates a space for connection and collaboration.

For over 30 years our employees have championed LGBTQ+ inclusion and together with our brands we are partnering to support employees and the wider community.
