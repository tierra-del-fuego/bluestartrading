OUR UNIQUE &
UNITED
WORKFORCE

Equality and inclusion is good for our business – broadening our ability to serve more consumers and driving market growth.  Simply put, it helps us win.

This is enabled by our efforts to attract, develop, and retain the best employees from the broadest pool of talent available to serve billions of diverse consumers around the world. Our unique differences spark innovation and creativity, inspire our careers and our culture, and help our company and our people to grow. We are united by our goal to be a force for growth and a force for good. We are Unique. We are United. We are P&G.
