GLOBAL WORKFORCE

BUSINESS & TECHNICAL
ASSOCIATES

We are committed to building a superior employee experience for our Business and Technical Associates (BTAs), as we are for all employees. By understanding their unique perspectives and needs, we can increase their sense of belonging, foster continued learning and development, tailor benefits and plans, and ultimately enable the growth of each individual, our organization and business.

Bala Purushothaman

Chief Human Resources Officer

Building a culture of belonging takes effort at all levels of a company, so it’s important to connect with, understand and support Business and Technical Associates (BTAs).

From the office to the lab and the manufacturing floor, our BTAs bring unique perspectives that help drive the production of superior products our consumers love. P&G’s BTAs perform administrative, technical, and other important tasks that ensure our operational success around the globe.

To help create a workplace where all employees can thrive, we’ve partnered with Catalyst’s Frontline Employee Initiative. Building on our longstanding collaboration, this new initiative focuses on impactful and scalable solutions that improve the attraction, retention, and satisfaction of frontline employees.

At P&G, every voice has an opportunity to positively impact how we serve our consumers and ensure all our colleagues around the world can contribute to their fullest potential.
