A Portfolio of Daily-Use
Categories Where
Performance Drives
Brand Choice

P&G operates in 10 categories: Fabric Care, Home Care, Baby Care, Feminine Care, Family Care, Hair Care, Skin & Personal Care, Oral Care, Personal Health Care, and Grooming.

In each of these daily-use categories, where performance drives brand choice, we continue to increase the superiority of our offerings.

Ten Categories Organized in Five Operating Sectors

P&G has a focused portfolio of daily-use products in categories where performance plays a significant role in brand choice. Our focus is on delivering irresistibly superior products with the best performance and value, to delight consumers at a price that is considered worth it across each price tier where the brand is offered.
