Transform Cleaning with P&G's Superior Home Care Innovations: Faster, Easier, Better

Discover how P&G’s offerings deliver a superior clean and give you more time.

Keeping a clean home can often feel like a never-ending chore. But what if your cleaning routine could be easier and more efficient, leaving you with more time to do the activities you love? Good news! We are focused on providing you with superior products designed to transform your cleaning routine, simplify your chores and give you back the time needed to do other interests.

Brands to Get You Started

Laundry Day Just Got Easier: Conquer Laundry with Tide Power PODS® with Downy

Mountains of laundry piling up? Tide Power PODS® with Downy has you covered. These convenient pods pack a powerful 4-in-1 punch: detergent, stain remover, brightener and Downy® Soft Boosters. Simply toss a pod in, add your laundry, set it to cold water and let it work its magic.

Power PODS® feature 50% more cleaning power than Tide original liquid detergent for a better clean. And, since they’re #1 at removing stains and odors verus the average laundry packs, you can trust them to clean and freshen up everything from beloved blankets and sports jerseys to tablecloths, guestroom linens and even your labrador’s bedding. Fresh, clean laundry is a simple pleasure, and the convenience factor frees up valuable time for the things you enjoy.

Skip the Pre-Rinse: Sparkling Dishes with Cascade Platinum Plus

Nearly half of us miss out on personal time because of dishwasher failure, needing to prewash, rewash or manually dry dishes, according to a P&G survey. Cascade Platinum Plus eliminates the need for these extra, time-consuming steps so you can skip the prework and rework. Just scrape off remaining food scraps, load the dishwasher and you’re done.

The pre-measured pods feature a dual-power cleaning formula with 50% more protein-fighting enzymes to break down protein-rich foods, twice the amount of a proprietary enzyme to address starches and two times the grease cleaning power of Dawn to remove grease, deliver improved shine and help dry dishes. As a result, dishes come out clean without pre-rinsing, pre-washing, re-washing or hand-drying. With Cascade Platinum Plus, dishes can go straight from the table, to the dishwasher and to the cabinet so you can spend less time at the sink and more time doing the things you enjoy. This game-changing solution saves time and effort, so embrace convenience and let the dishwasher do the work for you.

Effortless Floor Cleaning: Swiffer PowerMop for the Win

People told us that cleaning the floor is one of the most tedious parts of their cleaning routine. With spills, foot traffic and pet hair, it can feel like a constant battle. But many people still lug around a traditional mop and bucket, thinking it’s the only way to get a deep clean.

The Swiffer PowerMop offers a faster, easier solution. The all-in-one cleaning system features over 300 powerful scrubbing strips and a looped design that captures loose crumbs and breaks down tough sticky messes. Its sleek, modern design and 360-degree swivel head reaches tight spaces, making it easier to maneuver even under furniture and along baseboards. And the fast-drying cleaning solution leaves floors virtually streak- and residue-free and shining. Perfect for quick cleanups or a thorough cleaning, the Swiffer PowerMop simplifies floor care, giving you a superior clean in half the time as a mop and bucket.

We understand the challenges you face — whether it’s everyday cleaning, preparing your home for guests or doing a deep reset. Our advanced products not only deliver superior cleaning results but also enhance your cleaning routines, saving you valuable time and effort. What’s not to love about a cleaner home and more free time!
