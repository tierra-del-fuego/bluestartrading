Working With Partners To Expand Our Reach And Impact.

Collaborating for Impact

We develop and nurture long standing and transparent partnerships that enable our brands to have sustained impact that helps economies grow, businesses thrive, and communities flourish.

Making Prom Night Perfect

P&G brands, including Herbal Essences, Head & Shoulders, Olay and Gillette, partnered with Perkins School for the Blind to provide prom-ready products and accessible grooming services for their graduating students.

Supporting New Storytellers

P&G, Queen Latifah, Flavor Unit and Tribeca Studios partnered to create The Queen Collective, a program providing mentorship, production and distribution support to multicultural filmmakers, broadening the advertising landscape in TV, film and media.

Can’t Cancel Pride

Now in it's fifth year, Can't Cancel Pride was created by P&G in June 2020 to support the LGBTQ+ community during the COVID-19 pandemic. Can’t Cancel Pride brings together the music and entertainment community to raise visibility and funds for six LGBTQ+ organizations - GLAAD, The Trevor Project, SAGE, Outright International, National Black Justice Collective and CenterLink.

Keeping Girls in School

Always partnered with actress and choreographer Bontle Modiselle to create the #BloodSisters dance to raise awareness about period stigma and promote confidence in girls in South Africa.
